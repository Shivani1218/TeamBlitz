Prevention

As we all know Prevention is better than cure By following the measures given below you'll be able to protect yourself and your loved ones too

Prevention and measurements are important to prevent the disease to spread, for every individual even if their immune system is strongly boosted, it may not affect you but you can be its carrier. These precautions are important to be taken into consideration not only by uninfected people but also by infected ones.

Here's what you could do as an indivdual:

  • Clean your hands properly.
  • Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.
  • Maintain safe distance of minimum 1m with everyone.
  • Wear a mask.
  • Avoid touching your eyes, nose or mouth.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell.
  • Avoid social gatherings.
  • Always carry a hand sanitizer.

  • Plan and identify the employees that need to come to work while others can continue to work from home.
  • Rearrange seating at work stations, canteens, and common places as back-to-back to incorporate 1-metre physical distance.
  • Implement or enhance shift or split-team arrangements, encourage teleworking.
  • Minimize the need for physical meetings with teleconferencing.
  • Suspend workplace events and social gatherings.
  • Set up a good ventilation system to purify air.

  • To limit exposure, only go out when it is absolutely necessary and limit your total number of trips.
  • Maintain a social distance of 6 feet or more from people around you.
  • Carry hand sanitizer. Cleaning wipes, tissues and a clean mask.
  • Avoid touching door handles, elevator buttons and handrails.

Arogya Setu App

Aarogya Setu is an Indian open-source COVID-19 "Contact tracing, Syndromic mapping and Self-assessment" digital service, primarily a mobile app, developed by the National Informatics Centre under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY).

The new coronavirus tracking app warns users if they have crossed paths with any infected people recently.The app is available on both iOS and Android can be downloaded via respective app stores.
