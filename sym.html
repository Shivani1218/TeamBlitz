Symptoms

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

Although the below listed symptoms are an indicative of the presence of the virus ,it should be understood that none of them confirm the Covid-19 positive status of the patient. The patient must immediately get themselves tested at the nearest hospital.

These are the most common symptoms.Although it might just be another regular flu, it is important that the person isolates himself from the general crowd. Most common symptoms:
    Most common Symptoms:
  • fever
  • dry cough
  • tiredness

These symptoms are slightly severe and the patient experiencing such symptoms must seek help and get themselves tested. Although there is no need to panic, by taking precautions now further severities can be prevented.

  • aches and pains
  • sore throat
  • diarrhoea
  • conjunctivitis
  • headache
  • loss of taste or smell
  • a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes

    • If such symptoms are seen the patient must be rushed to the nearest hospital and must be given immediate treatment. It can be fatal if the patient is not treated immmediately.

    Serious symptoms:
  • difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • chest pain or pressure
  • loss of speech or movement

    • Note: If symptoms are persistent please visit your nearest hospital and get yourself tested. If the symptoms are severe then rush to your nearest hospital and seek immediate help. Remember even a minute is crucial to save a life.
